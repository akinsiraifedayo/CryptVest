Account Funding

Get help with funding your CryptVest account

You can fund your CryptVest account using cryptocurrency deposits. Once the deposit is confirmed on the blockchain, your account balance will reflect the added funds.

Deposits made via cryptocurrency usually take between 10-30 minutes depending on network traffic.

Withdrawals

Help with withdrawing your investments

You can withdraw your funds by navigating to the "Withdraw" section in your dashboard. Withdrawals can be made in cryptocurrency.

Withdrawal fees vary based on the method used. Cryptocurrency withdrawals are subject to blockchain network fees + a security fee.

Security & Authentication

Stay secure on CryptVest

To keep your pass phrase secure, write it on a sheet of paper or store it somewhere where you would never lose it. This adds an extra layer of security to your CryptVest account.

My Investments

Track and manage your cryptocurrency investments

You can track the performance of your portfolio in the "Investments" section of your dashboard. Real-time updates on your cryptocurrency holdings, price changes, and overall portfolio value are provided.

Referrals & Bonuses

Maximize rewards by inviting others to CryptVest

The CryptVest referral program allows you to earn rewards for every user who signs up using your unique referral link and makes their first deposit. Bonuses are credited in cryptocurrency.
